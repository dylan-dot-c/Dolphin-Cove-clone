How to Get to Dolphin Cove Moon Palace Jamica?

The Moon Palace Jamaica all-inclusive resort is in the Ocho Ríos area. However, we recommend you fly to the Montego Bay International Airport (MBJ), located 86 km (53 mi) away.

At the airport, you will find many shuttles or private transfers to your hotel if you didn’t book it in advance.
