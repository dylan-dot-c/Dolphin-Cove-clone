How to Get to Dolphin Cove Puerto Seco?

Dolphin Cove Puerto Seco is in the Discovery Bay area, 63 km(39 mi) from the Sangster International Airport in Montego Bay.

Getting to Puerto Seco Beach is effortless from any villa or hotel in Discovery Bay. Hop on a local bus or get the “route taxi” in the surrounding towns through A1 Highway.
