Dolphin Cove Ocho Rios

Ocho Ríos is a quintessential city that welcomes cruise travelers with white sandy beaches and swaying palm trees, where Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos, a fantastic Dolphin Habitat and natural park framed by majestic lush tropical nature, is located.

Perfectly appointed in the northeast area of Jamaica and only 4 km from the Cruise Terminal at Turtle Beach Road, Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos is a superb option to start your vacation in Jamaica.

From the very first moment you step into Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos, you will feel the Jamaican vibe with a sumptuous natural backdrop to kick back in the pool or delight with traditional and freshly brewed Jamaican coffee.

Unleash your adventurous spirit and get ready to enjoy one of the most fantastic experiences in a swim with Dolphins in Ocho Rios and exploring this extraordinary natural park in Jamaica.

With your interactive program paid admission at Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos, you get:

  • Jungle trail.
  • Lunch.
  • Cawabunga water slide.
  • Kayaks.
  • Stingrays interactive program.
  • Beach Club.
  • Dunn's River Falls Experience (swimming at the river falls and cultural activities).
  • Activities with the marine species of your choice in their natural environment.
  • Lifejackets.
  • Use of facilities and showers.

During your visit to Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos, before or after your interactive program with Dolphins or sharks, you will enjoy the natural paths across the park where nature meets sun-and-fun moments.

Discover the most incredible tropical species, including iguanas and birds, and a great diversity of flowers and colorful trees.

Get prepared to indulge your senses in a tropical scene and have an unforgettable time with your loved ones in an idyllic place like Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos in Jamaica!
